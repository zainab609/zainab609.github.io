How we believe we have met the expectations for this project:

  1. The site has five pages, including index.html, materials.html, instructions.html, about.html, and other.html
  2. Each page has the same favicon
  3. Each page has the title of “How to Make Origami Lucky Stars”
  4. Each page has a navigation bar leading to the other pages under the header
  5. Images resize and the navigation bar turns into a column when the display becomes smaller
  6. Each page includes at least one image with appropriate alt text, such as “Close up photo of origami stars”
  7. None of the pages have any WAVE or Nu Validator errors or warnings.
  8. The header and the rest of the pages have a different font face.
  9. There is a form on the bottom of other.html.
  10. other.html and materials.html both have links to outside websites.
  11. The site is available at https://zainab609.github.io/343-s23-p1
  12. https://zainab609.github.io/343-s23-p1/report.html contains this report and a PDF of the design sketch
